# What would you do if a critical bug appears in production?

Handling critical bugs is a test of your problem-solving skills. Interviewers want to know if you can respond quickly and effectively.

## Why Interviewers Ask This Question

- To check your crisis management skills
- To see if you can minimize impact and communicate well

## Steps to Take

- Acknowledge and assess the issue
- Communicate with stakeholders
- Roll back or hotfix if possible
- Investigate root cause
- Add tests to prevent recurrence

## Example

- Use monitoring tools to detect issues
- Deploy a rollback or patch

## Interview Tips

- Mention communication and documentation
- Discuss post-mortem and prevention

## Useful English Phrases & Sentence Starters

- If a critical bug appears, I ...
- I communicate with ...
- For example, ...

## Sample Answer

> If a critical bug appears in production, I immediately communicate with the team and stakeholders, assess the impact, and roll back or hotfix if possible. I then investigate the root cause and add tests to prevent it from happening again.
